About Eat Beans

Eat Beans is inspired by the iconic arcade game that took the world by storm in the 1980s. This browser version brings all the excitement of the original maze-action game to your device.

The concept is simple yet addictive: guide your character through a maze, eating all the beans while avoiding colorful ghosts. Special power pellets allow you to turn the tables and hunt the ghosts for a limited time.

This online version preserves the charm of the original while optimizing the experience for modern web browsers. Whether you're feeling nostalgic or discovering this classic for the first time, Eat Beans offers endless entertainment for players of all ages.
